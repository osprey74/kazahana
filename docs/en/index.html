Features

Four reasons kazahana makes your daily Bluesky experience better

memory

Lightweight

Uses your OS's native WebView to dramatically reduce memory usage compared to PWA. Your PC stays fast, even after hours of use.

bolt

Fast

Native app startup speed. Smooth timeline loading with no waiting — browse your feed without friction.

dashboard

Simple

A clean single-column UI. Timeline, posting, notifications, search, and profile — everything you need, nothing you don't.

translate

Multilingual

Supports 11 languages including English and Japanese. Bluesky users around the world can use kazahana in their native language.

What You Can Do

Everything available in kazahana

dynamic_feed Auto-refreshing timeline (configurable interval)
bookmark Reading position marker
edit_square Post & Reply
favorite Like & Repost
image Image attachments (up to 4, with ALT text)
zoom_in Image lightbox
link Link card (OGP preview)
forum Thread view
notifications Notifications (like, repost, follow, mention, reply, quote)
person Profile & Follow / Unfollow
thumb_up Liked posts
photo_library Media posts
search Post & user search
dark_mode Dark / Light / System theme
translate 11 languages
shield Content moderation (labels, blur, settings)
tab Custom feeds & list feeds
lock Threadgate & postgate (reply/quote restrictions)
format_quote Quote post
bookmarks Bookmarks
delete Post deletion
refresh Manual reload (tab click / F5 / button)
vertical_align_top System tray
